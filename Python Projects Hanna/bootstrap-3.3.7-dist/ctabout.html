About Us

Mission Statement

The Carrot Top Foundation for Crummy Comedians (CTFCC) hopes to find a cure for atrocious comedians around the world. We have pinpointed the cause of unfunniness, a small defect in the funny bone. Our mission is to fund the operation and treatment for these comedians, and hopefully remove the dangerous bone that has so greatly affected their lives. This will mean that their careers as comedians will be over, but with the help of our lovely donors we will be able to provide these people with enough money to live the rest of their lives in comfort, and off the blessed stage which belongs to the true comedians.
